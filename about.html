Our Story

Since our first day in 1978, our mission is to make innovative material solutions accessible for everyone. We strive to lead in the chemical goods industry built on a passion for innovating chemicals for textile, leather, and more. Our vertically integrated structure guarantees efficient production, offering a wide array of material solutions to grow manufacturers around the globe.

Kagi Chemie believes in a vision beyond profits. We support sustainable and safe methods of production that reduce environmental degradation, maintain the productivity of the land over time, and back the safety and health of our employees. We rigorously comply with environmental regulations and are ever developing our operating procedures to make a positive impact.

At Kagi Chemie, we will continue to take on challenges compassionately and prosper with our business partners.
