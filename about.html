About Us

Introduction to KAGI CHEMIE

Founded in 1978, Kagi Chemie is a well-known company that manufactures chemicals used in textile, leather and other industrial uses. Our products have been sold to internationally.

We value our employee's health, safety and benefits. Our factory is cleaned routinely with modernized equipments, such as autoclaves that provides precise controllings and automated controlling systems. With these, we are able to ensure the stability of our product quality.

For years, Kagi Chemie maintains great credibility, stable quality and gives our best service to clients. We are grateful that Kagi Chemie is trusted by many clients so that we do our best to contribute to the society.

Our Core Values

Integrity has been the key of our business since establishment. We consider honesty as the most important value to be honored as the top priority when servicing customers!

Caring for others is honored as one of the basic values for humanity. Kagi Chemie put ourselves into other's shoe, care for others' wellness and our mother nature. Till now, we are still doing our best to make this world a better place, take up the responsibility of being a global citizen.

Innovation

Innovation is all around us and never stop its path to prosperous. We dare to try and take on challenges, keep movitate and improve ourselves. Kagi Chemie always search for reliable projects and methodology for operation, product innovation and mindsets to give better products!

Service
