Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help? Find the help you need below!

Order

Orders During Workdays

If you happen to order our products before noon (GMT+8), we can complete your order at the earliest by the end of the day, with your order shipped to you by the following day. If you order our products in the afternoon (GMT +8), we will process your order as soon as possible and have it completed at the soonest by the end of tomorrow, with shipment arranged and get the products to you by the end of the following day.

However, please be adviced that the actual time of product arrival depends on the logistics company and the actual situation during the shipment. We thank you for your patience and the trust into us!

Orders During Holidays

For orders placed during holidays in Taiwan (GMT +8), your orders will be processed in the next workday as soon as possible. Please wait patiently and we apologize for any inconvience caused.

Return or Exchange

No Defect In Products

If you would like to return the products, but the quality of the products appeared to be normal after our tests and confirmation, due to there are no issues with our products, we apologize for not accepting return / exchange / refund of any form.

Defects In Products

However, if you encounter defects in our products during your production, please contact us as soon as possible. If the products are to be defective after our tests and confirmation, with the defects are confirmed not be caused by human operations of any kind, we accept exchanges of products within 7 days after the initial shipment.

Still got questions?

We are here to answer them. Get in touch with us via the contact section!
